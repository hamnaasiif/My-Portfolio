My Journey

01

I'm a final-year Computer Science student at FAST-NUCES with a focused interest in frontend development and user interface design. My foundation lies in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and React, with practical experience building responsive, component-based interfaces. Notably, I developed a full-stack Art Gallery Web Application that integrates secure authentication, image handling, and modern design principles to deliver a seamless user experience.

02

Beyond the frontend, I've explored systems and backend development using C++ and Python — through projects like a compiler, a GUI-based operating system, and a hybrid P2P communication app. These experiences ground my design decisions in technical understanding. I often carry over the discipline and visual sensibility I've developed through traditional art, aiming to create interfaces that are not just functional, but thoughtful and visually composed.

03

Currently, I'm expanding into the MERN stack and computer vision to broaden my capabilities. My goal is to bridge the gap between technical excellence and creative expression, creating digital experiences that are both powerful and beautiful.
